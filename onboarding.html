Step 1

Your details

Just the basics and access we need to get started.

Step 2

Voice memo

Record yourself answering the questions below. Talk naturally — this is how we learn your voice, your audience, and what offer will actually work for them. Most people finish in 10 to 15 minutes.

Answer these questions:

  • 1 What is your newsletter about and who is it for?
  • 2 How did your audience find you and why do they stay subscribed?
  • 3 What do your subscribers most often ask you about or thank you for?
  • 4 Have you ever sold anything to your list before? What happened?
  • 5 What would you love to sell them if you had the right words for it?
  • 6 Who are the people on your list — what do they do, what are they trying to achieve?
  • 7 What does success look like for you in the next 60 days?
Once recorded, send the file to amon@sevenmedia.space or via WhatsApp. We transcribe it — you do not need to write anything out.
Step 3

Content assets

Create a shared Google Drive folder and drop in whatever you have. Share it with amon@sevenmedia.space and paste the link below.

  • Your best 3 to 5 newsletter issues
  • Photos of yourself (used in emails and assets)
  • Any results, wins, or proof screenshots
  • Logo or brand colors if you have them
  • Links to YouTube videos, reels, or podcasts
Optional

Anything else

Anything you want us to know before we start. Past attempts, things that have not worked, specific things you want the emails to avoid.

Questions before you start? Message Amon at amon@sevenmedia.space
